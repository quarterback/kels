# Zaryanova
*Formerly the colony of **Kiralina**. A wealthy, Black-majority Pacific great power occupying most
of what is otherwise the Russian Far East. Founder-ratified canon; supersedes earlier drafts.*

## High concept

Zaryanova is one of the world's great powers — economically comparable to China, more influential
in the Pacific than South Korea. Not Wakanda-style Afrofuturism: it is an alternate path of
**modernity built on ports, migration, infrastructure, education, public transit, maritime commerce,
and multiculturalism**.

The defining question is not "what if there were a Black country in Asia?" but: **what would
modernity look like if one of the world's great powers had been built by descendants of
African-Americans on the Pacific Rim?**

## Founding

**1850.** **Isaiah Thompson**, an African-American intellectual educated in Europe, convinced
**Tsar Alexander II** to establish an autonomous homeland in the Russian Far East.

Russian motivations: populate the Far East, develop its resources, embarrass the United States, and
showcase Russian "progressivism."

Ships departed largely from **San Francisco**, carrying settlers from the American South —
particularly the Carolinas. The first colony was called **Kiralina**, a Russian colonial name
derived from "Carolina." Kiralina remained the colony's name; **Zaryanova** became the name of the
independent republic.

## Territory

- Primorsky Krai
- Khabarovsk Krai
- Sakhalin Oblast
- Kamchatka Krai
- Magadan Oblast
- Jewish Autonomous Oblast
- the Kuril Islands

**~2,022,000 km² (780,783 sq mi) — the 38th largest country in the world.** An enormous Pacific
coastline. Spans five time zones, UTC+9 to UTC+13. *(Chukotka is NOT Zaryanovan — the seven
territories above are the whole country.)*

*(Alaska is NOT Zaryanovan.)*

## Population

**29,863,010.** Far larger than the real Russian Far East, the result of several Great Migrations,
internal development, the oil and gas boom, immigration, very high quality of life, and sustained
strategic investment.

Large enough to support multiple megaregions, global corporations, major sports leagues, a domestic
gaming industry, a AAA film industry, a significant military, and a large internal consumer market.

## Cities

**Gannibal** — the capital, named for **Abram Petrovich Gannibal**. A purpose-built national
capital: Russian imperial core, Soviet civic districts, American street grid, Nordic public realm,
a Dubai-scale modern skyline, Pacific harbour city, extensive transit, floating districts,
monumental public architecture.

**Pushkin** — the largest city and the commercial and financial capital. The largest metropolitan
area, and more cosmopolitan than Gannibal.

Other provincial cities are being renamed from their Russian counterparts.

## People, language, religion

Core population: **African-American descendants**. Major minorities: Russians, Koreans, Chinese.
Later immigration from Japan, Southeast Asia, Europe, and the United States. Multi-racial, but
culturally anchored in the **Afro-Russian** experience.

**Official languages: English and Russian.** Korean and Chinese widely spoken. Over generations a
distinct **English creole written primarily in Cyrillic** developed.

Religion: Far East Baptist Church 44% · Russian Orthodox 35% · other Protestant 12% · Jewish 2% ·
other/none 7%.

## Economy

One of the world's richest countries. Offshore oil and LNG transformed it from the late 20th
century. Comparable to Norway, the UAE, Singapore, or South Korea — but on a far larger landmass.

Industries: shipping, ports, fishing, LNG, offshore oil, rare earths, finance, technology. Now
diversified into AI, higher education, maritime engineering, logistics, biotech, and advanced
manufacturing. Resource revenue is held offshore in a sovereign wealth fund (Norwegian model).

**Locals-first ownership:** foreign property ownership is permitted only in designated regions,
registered and taxed with high recurring non-local fees; foreigners pay markedly higher rents than
locals. Foreign capital is welcomed but premium-gated.

## History

**WWII** — Zaryanova became one of the principal logistics hubs of the Pacific, supplying Soviet
forces, Allied forces, and Pacific convoys. American, Australian, and British personnel rotated
through its ports, accelerating jazz, American music, industrial development, aviation, and
infrastructure.

**The Civil Rights era** — the 1950s and 60s produced another Great Migration, as thousands of
African-Americans relocated after becoming disillusioned with progress in the United States. The
movement caused Zaryan citizens to question Russian influence, national identity, independence, and
democracy — producing constitutional reforms that expanded civil liberties and strengthened
parliamentary government.

## Diplomacy — "The Pacific Switzerland"

Neutral diplomacy, international arbitration, peace summits, financial regulation, global banking,
sovereign wealth funds. Frequently hosts Korea talks, China-Pacific negotiations, and Arctic
conferences.

- **Russia** — founding patron; the "homeland" relationship, deep energy ties. Zaryanova has
  economically outgrown it; the relationship carries Russian paternalism against a Zaryan drive for
  peer status.
- **China** — critical land-border neighbour and trade partner; a live domestic wedge issue between
  government and opposition.
- **Japan** — largest trade partner and principal defence-industrial partner.
- **India** — the fastest-growing partnership; a major Indian commercial and tech district in the
  capital.
- **United States** — cyclically frosty and friendly; founded as a rebuke to American racism yet
  culturally descended from it.
- **DPRK** — handled through the border town of **Vorota** ("Gateway City"): a managed defector
  pipeline and elite shopping corridor.

Non-nuclear.

## Culture

**Design philosophy: Opal Futurism** — neither cyberpunk nor solarpunk. Iridescent materials;
turquoise, coral, amber, lavender; polished concrete and ceramic composites; public infrastructure;
ecological cities; floating architecture; transit-first urbanism. Visual influences: Helsinki,
Singapore, Seoul, Vladivostok, Hong Kong — not New York or Los Angeles.

**Entertainment** — instead of medieval-European fantasy, Zaryan games feature ports, floating
cities, couriers, engineers, harbour workers, weather control, and ocean exploration. Bright Pacific
palette, 16-bit pixel art, Afro-Asian protagonists, ecological futurism.

**Ottelun** — the national beverage. Created because coffee was near-impossible to import in the
early colonial years; settlers blended locally grown black tea with imported Milk Oolong. ("Milk"
refers to the oolong's naturally creamy character, not dairy.) Ordering **"chai milk"** almost
always means Ottelun. Served hot, iced, flavoured, sparkling, and as boba.

**Dalski** — the national spirit, from Russian *дальний* ("far"). A clear distilled spirit from
local ingredients; the equivalent of soju, vodka, or shochu.

**Universities** — world-leading research institutions, known for migration studies,
multiculturalism, AI, ocean engineering, sustainable extraction, and infrastructure design.

## Place in this universe

Not a Nelôxi-world member — its axis is Russia, China, Japan, India. Two connections matter:

1. **Kinship with Valdória.** Both are 19th-century Black-American emigration projects that became
   prosperous sovereign states — Valdória's Freedmen Homestead in the Atlantic, Zaryanova's Tsarist
   grant in the Pacific. The same impulse, opposite ends of the earth; the two republics recognise
   each other as siblings, with cultural and migratory ties between them.
2. **Evidence for the world's thesis.** Zaryanova is part of why the United States is diminished in
   this timeline — one of several destinations that absorbed the talent and people America refused.
