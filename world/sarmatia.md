# Sarmatia — the People's Republic of Sarmatia

*Formerly **East Neloxia** (the independence-era name). Short name **Sarmatia**; demonym
**Sarmat**; adjective **Sarmatian**; Nelôxi **Sarmatô**. Founder-ratified.*

## What it is

A sovereign state, Neloxia-aligned, running from the Black Sea to the edge of the Iranian
plateau. It separated from Neloxia amicably — too much land to administer from Kunislinnô,
and rich enough to stand alone. It was **East Neloxia** at independence and took the name
**Sarmatia** after.

The name solves the problem the state has: it spans Circassians, Ossetians, Chechens,
Dagestanis, Kalmyks, Kazakhs, Turkmens, and Khorasanis, so it cannot be named for any of
them. **Sarmatia** is the classical umbrella for exactly this steppe-and-Caucasus zone — old
enough that no living people can claim it against the others. **People's Republic** does the
same work in the political register: it names no titular nationality.

Its shape is a trade route made political: the northern/Caspian branch of the Silk Road, held
end to end. Every holding is a bead on that one string — Genoese **Caffa** (Crimea) at the
Black Sea mouth, the Caucasus gate, **Astrakhan** at the Volga–Caspian hinge, the Caspian east
shore, and **Herat** as the great inland exchange.

## Language

Its own Nelôxi standard — the sixth fork (see `BRIEF-eastfork-for-agent.md`). The only fork
where the Finnic element is not the grammatical lead:

- **Vocabulary:** Nelôxi (the core lexicon, unchanged)
- **Grammar lead:** Adyghe (West Circassian) — SOV, ergative alignment, polysynthetic verb,
  verb-internal negation
- **Secondary Finnic partner:** Estonian — the noun-case morphology and the family thread
- **Loan quarry:** Turkic/Turkish — the Low German role; everyday/casual borrowed vocabulary
  (Turkic leads the loans, not the grammar, because Tarun next door owns the Turkic identity)
- **Script:** full Cyrillic (recommended — matches real Adyghe, echoes the Uusatôm overlay)

The corridor's other populations keep their own languages. Nelôxi is the business language of
the whole region — not English, not Russian.

## Relationships

- **Neloxia** — the parent. Amicable separation; deep alignment; the Kuwait condominium is
  held jointly (see `kuwait-condominium.md`).
- **Tarun** — the unified Turkic bloc on its northern/eastern flank. The corridor runs
  alongside Tarun's steppe routes; Turkic is Sarmatia's heaviest loan-source.
- **Qazania** — landlocked to the north; its Volga outlet runs through Sarmatian Astrakhan.
- **Kuwait** — the Gulf terminal at the far end of the corridor, co-held with Neloxia.
