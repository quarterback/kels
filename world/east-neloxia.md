# East Neloxia — the Corridor State

*Provisional name. Founder-ratified in substance; final name open (candidates: Šaraya, Kervania,
Colchia/Kôlxônô, Sarmatia, Alania — or it keeps "East Neloxia").*

## What it is

A sovereign state, Neloxia-aligned, running from the Black Sea to the edge of the Iranian plateau.
It separated from Neloxia amicably — too much land to administer from Kunislinnô, and rich enough
to stand alone. "East Neloxia" is its independence-era name.

Its shape is a trade route made political: the northern/Caspian branch of the Silk Road, held end
to end. Every holding is a bead on that one string — Genoese Caffa (Crimea) at the Black Sea
mouth, the Caucasus gate, Astrakhan at the Volga-Caspian hinge, the Caspian east shore, and Herat
as the great inland exchange.

## Language

Its own Nelôxi standard — the sixth fork (see BRIEF-eastfork-for-agent.md). The only fork where
the Finnic element is not the grammatical lead:

- **Vocabulary:** Nelôxi (the core lexicon, unchanged)
- **Grammar lead:** Adyghe (West Circassian) — SOV, ergative alignment, polysynthetic verb,
  verb-internal negation
- **Secondary Finnic partner:** Estonian — the noun-case morphology and the family thread
- **Loan quarry:** Turkic/Turkish — the Low German role; everyday/casual borrowed vocabulary
  (Turkic leads the loans, not the grammar, because Tarun next door owns the Turkic identity)
- **Script:** full Cyrillic (recommended — matches real Adyghe, echoes the Uusatôm overlay)

The corridor's other populations keep their own languages. Nelôxi is the business language of the
whole region — not English, not Russian.

## Relationships

- **Neloxia** — the parent. Amicable separation; deep alignment; the Kuwait condominium is held
  jointly (see world/kuwait-condominium.md).
- **Tarun** — the unified Turkic bloc on its northern/eastern flank. The corridor runs alongside
  Tarun's steppe routes; Turkic is East Neloxia's heaviest loan-source.
- **Qazania** — landlocked to the north; its Volga outlet runs through East Neloxian Astrakhan.
- **Kuwait** — the Gulf terminal at the far end of the corridor, co-held with Neloxia.
