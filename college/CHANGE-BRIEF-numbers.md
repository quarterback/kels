# Nelôxi Numbers — current system (v4.x)

Standard Nelôxi counts **base-12 (dozenal)** in Slavic-derived digits. The old Finnic and
Germanic number systems are extinct canon (see archive/extinct-numbers.md); the inland dialect
still counts the old Finnic way.

Digits: **nolô** 0 · **jedôn** 1 · **dva** 2 · **tri** 3 · **xtiri** 4 · **peñç** 5 · **xeç** 6 ·
**sedôm** 7 · **osôm** 8 · **deveñç** 9 · **deseñç** 10 · **elva** 11 · **düna** 12 (round).

Teens 13–23 are fused single words: dünjôn 13, dünva 14, düntri 15, dünxtir 16, dünpeñç 17,
dünxeç 18, dünsedm 19, dünosm 20, dündevç 21, dündesç 22, dünelva 23.

Above: bare juxtaposition, largest-first, no connector, no hyphen — *dva düna tri* = 27.
Breakpoints: **düna** 12, **grosô** 144, **mīrô** 1728, **miljôn** ~12⁶.
Ordinals: **-tô** (jedôntô, dvatô, tritô). Fractions from the trade quarry: **half**, **dēl**,
number+dēl (*tri-dēl* "a third").
