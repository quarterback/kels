## Submission & merge

Standard delegate format (Headword | PoS | Stratum | Register | Gloss | Example | Translation, plus
collision notes, 25–40 words, one domain, stated loan ratio). Everyday domains (body, building)
should NOT run 100% loan — distribute; genre/technical domains legitimately do (§27/§33). The Rector
reviews against HEAD, reshapes collisions and orthography, merges, regenerates `data/` and the
bundles, logs the ruling, and returns a rulings doc.
