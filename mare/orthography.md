# Maré Orthography — closed Oceanic extension

*The bounded spelling rule for Maré/Nengone material on the Nelôxian spine.*

Maré is the Oceanic standard of Nelôxi. It keeps a separate lexicon under `mare/`, but it
shares the common grammar and archive rules of `../saharannaise/COMMON-REGISTER.md`. Roots may
come from Maré/Nengone; inflection and derivation remain Nelôxian.

## 1 · What Maré may add

Maré adds **no new single-letter diacritics** to the mainland inventory. Its visible scars are
legal digraphs and apostrophe-marked consonants used by the Maré/Nengone quarry. They are
orthographic units, not imported grammar.

| Feature | Rule | Examples / notes |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Aspirate-nasal clusters** | **hm, hn, hng** are kept as fixed onset clusters. Do not split, simplify, or respell them. | *hm-*, *hn-*, *hng-* |
| **Labial and palatal breath digraphs** | **wh, hy** are kept where the source requires them. | no conversion to *w/y* without a ruling |
| **Fricative digraphs** | **sh, xh** are legal Maré digraphs. They are not the Saharannaise §47 **x** rule and are not evidence of retired *š*. | *sh* is not *š*; *xh* is a unit |
| **Affricate / dental digraphs** | **dr, th, tr** are legal Maré digraphs and remain as written in sanctioned roots. | no automatic reduction to *d/t/r* |
| **Velar nasal digraph** | **ng** is legal as a digraph. It is not Congolaise **ŋ** and must not be rewritten to it. | *ng* stays two letters |
| **Apostrophe-marked consonants** | **c', k', t'** are legal Maré consonant signs. The apostrophe is part of the root spelling, not punctuation to be stripped. | no bare *c/k/t* substitution unless ruled |
| **No extra vowel marks** | Maré uses the shared Nelôxian vowel inventory for archive forms; long vowels, where accepted, use macrons. | no new acute/grave/circumflex vowel series |

## 2 · Closed-extension rule

The extension is closed. New Maré forms may use only the shared Nelôxian spelling rules plus
the features in §1. A contributor may not add new graphemes, new diacritics, or private
source-language spellings by analogy. **Every new form that falls outside this table requires a
Maré Register ruling before it enters a lexicon.**

This follows the closed-extension precedent in `../congolaise/CONFLUENCE-PRINCIPLE.md`: the
standard may wear its source scars, but the scar set is finite and governed.
