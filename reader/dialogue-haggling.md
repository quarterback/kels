# Kalāstāji ja Ostaji — Kauplômus Markôtôl
## The Fisher and the Buyer — Haggling at the Market

*Corpus text, Nelôxi. Genre: haggling dialogue.*

---

**Kalāstāji:** Tēre! Kaçā, jõvā kalā! Värskē kalā, avuī pǟlt!
**Fisher:** Hello! Look, good fish! Fresh fish, from today!

**Ostaji:** Tēre. Kui mult maksā se kalā?
**Buyer:** Hello. How much does this fish cost?

**Kalāstāji:** Kalās prēu um dva rā. Agā sīnsīnele — kolm kalād dva rās eest!
**Fisher:** The fish's price is two money. But for you — three fish for two money!

**Ostaji:** Dva rā? Se um liig! Mä donā jedôn rā ja half.
**Buyer:** Two money? That's too much! I'll give one money and a half.

**Kalāstāji:** Jedôn rā ja half? Nalī! Kalā um värskē, äb vana. Jedôn rā ja dva dēl.
**Fisher:** One and a half? Nonsense! The fish is fresh, not old. One money and two parts.

**Ostaji:** Hǟ, olgu. Jedôn rā ja dva dēl. Agā lisā se petīt kalān tambē.
**Buyer:** Fine, okay. One money and two parts. But add that small fish too.

**Kalāstāji:** Sāli! Votā. Jõvā kauplômus!
**Fisher:** Deal! Take it. Good haggling!

**Ostaji:** Täno! Jõvā pǟvõ!
**Buyer:** Thank you! Good day!
