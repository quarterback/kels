# Pôlū Sup — Kuināmys
## Mushroom Soup — A Recipe

*Corpus text, Nelôxi. Genre: recipe / how-to.*

---

Votā pôlūn, sipôlôn, vôidôn, jôun, ja lēten.
Take mushroom, onion, butter, flour, and milk.

Puhādā pôlūn ja sipôlôn.
Clean the mushroom and onion.

Lôigā pôlūn ja sipôlôn petīt jagôks.
Cut the mushroom and onion into small pieces.

Sulatā vôidôn padāl.
Melt the butter in a pot.

Segā sipôlôn vôidôk. Keetā, kuni sipôl um pehmē.
Stir the onion with the butter. Cook until the onion is soft.

Lisā jôun ja segā. Tôl kallā lēten padālô. Keetā ja segā.
Add the flour and stir. Then pour the milk into the pot. Cook and stir.

Lisā pôlūn. Keetā, kuni sup um paks.
Add the mushroom. Cook until the soup is thick.

Asetā sōlan ja pipôrôn pǟl.
Put salt and pepper on top.

Kallā supôn platôlô. Menjā sojālt.
Pour the soup onto a plate. Eat while hot.
